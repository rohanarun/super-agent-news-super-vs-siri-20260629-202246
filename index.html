What Siri Is — and What It Isn’t

Siri’s strength

Recent WWDC coverage describes Siri’s long‑awaited AI overhaul, positioning it as a more conversational and capable assistant embedded deeply into iOS, macOS, CarPlay, and HomeKit. Reviews note it feels faster and more helpful in everyday contexts like driving or device control.

Siri’s limits

Siri remains primarily device‑centric. It excels at answering questions, controlling Apple services, and assisting within Apple’s guardrails — but it is not designed as a general computer‑use agent that can freely operate arbitrary desktops or repeat complex workflows.

Market context

Interest in personal AI agents is rising globally, with studies showing strong consumer demand. Platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Grok are also pushing agentic features, while tools such as Folk and Orchids explore relationship and workflow management from different angles.

How Super Takes a Different Approach

Why Computer Use Matters Now

News this month highlights that Google’s Gemini models can now operate computers directly — a milestone that also raises new security discussions.
